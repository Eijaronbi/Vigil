How it works

A four-part pipeline designed for clarity.

Part One

Collect from every channel

WhatsApp through a Chrome extension. Telegram through its Bot API. Twitter via Jina Reader. Gmail through OAuth. All read-only.

Part Two

Classify with hybrid AI

Keyword and sender rules catch important messages instantly. An LLM scores the rest for nuance. You control the threshold.

Part Three

Alert on your terms

Telegram for immediate push. Email for a daily digest. Voice readout for priority items.

Part Four

Verify on Monad

Every alert hash is stored immutably on Monad testnet. Anyone can verify.
