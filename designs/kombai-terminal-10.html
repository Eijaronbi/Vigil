Protocol

How It Works

Four operations. One pipeline.

01_connect

Connect

Authenticate your messaging accounts through secure OAuth. Vigil maintains persistent connections to WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter, and Gmail.

02_classify

Classify

Every message passes through the AI classifier — urgency scoring, spam filtering, sender reputation. It knows what matters to you.

03_alert

Alert

High-priority messages trigger voice alerts through your speakers. Say "check messages" to hear a summary of everything you missed.

04_attest

Attest

Critical messages are hashed and attested on Monad testnet. Tamper-proof records with on-chain timestamps. Public. Permanent.
