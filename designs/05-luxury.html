How it works

Connect once. Set your preferences. The system handles the rest.

I
Connect your sources WhatsApp via Chrome extension. Telegram via bot. Twitter and Gmail through their APIs. Each integration is read-only.
II
Define what matters Set keyword rules, priority senders, or topic filters. The AI scores the rest.
III
Receive alerts Telegram for immediate push. Email for daily digest. Voice for priority items.
IV
Verified on Monad Every alert hash is stored immutably. Anyone can verify.
