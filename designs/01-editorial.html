How it works

Connect once. Set your rules. Everything else is automatic.

Connect
Plug in your sources WhatsApp via Chrome extension. Telegram via a bot. Twitter via handle. Gmail via OAuth. Each one is read-only.
Classify
Rules, then AI Keywords and sender rules catch instantly. The LLM scores for nuance. You control the threshold.
Alert
Push, digest, or voice Telegram for immediate alerts. Email for a daily summary. Voice readout for priorities.
Verify
On-chain attestation Every important alert gets hashed and stored on Monad testnet. Public. Permanent. Verifiable.
