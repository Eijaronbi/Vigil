How It Works

A pipeline designed for clarity and speed.

Step 01

Connect

Secure OAuth links to your messaging accounts. Persistent WebSocket listeners maintain real-time awareness.

Step 02

Classify

Every message is scored by the AI classifier — urgency, sender reputation, content analysis, spam probability.

Step 03

Alert

High-priority messages trigger voice alerts. The "check messages" voice command reads your queue out loud.

Step 04

Attest

Critical messages are hashed and stored on the Monad testnet. Permanent timestamps with tamper-proof integrity.
