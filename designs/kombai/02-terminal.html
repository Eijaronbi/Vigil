Protocol

How It Works

Four operations. One pipeline.

01_connect

Connect

Authenticate your messaging accounts through secure OAuth. Vigil maintains persistent WebSocket connections to each source.

02_classify

Classify

Every message passes through the AI classifier — urgency scoring, spam filtering, sender reputation analysis.

03_alert

Alert

High-priority messages trigger voice alerts. Speak "check messages" to hear a summary of what you missed.

04_attest

Attest

Critical messages are hashed and attested on Monad testnet. Tamper-proof records with on-chain timestamps.
