Process

How It Works

From ingestion to immutable record.

Step 01

Connect

Link your messaging accounts through secure OAuth. Vigil establishes persistent listeners on each channel.

Step 02

Classify

Each message is analyzed by the AI classifier — urgency scoring, sender reputation, content analysis, and spam filtering.

Step 03

Alert

High-priority messages trigger voice alerts. Say "check messages" to hear a spoken summary of your queue.

Step 04

Attest

Critical messages are hashed and attested on the Monad testnet. Immutable timestamps, permanent proof.
