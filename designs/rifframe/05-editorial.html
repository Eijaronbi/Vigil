Voice alerts that respect your attention

Not every message needs a notification. Vigil's AI classifies before alerting — only priority messages trigger voice alerts through your headphones or speakers. The "check messages" command reads your queue on demand, organized by source and urgency.

Quiet when you need it. Loud when it matters.

Why on-chain attestation matters

Every classified message gets a hash stored on Monad testnet. This creates a tamper-proof chain of custody — verifiable by anyone, anytime. For compliance teams, auditors, and incident response, it means provable receipts without the overhead.
