How it works

A four-stage pipeline that collects, classifies, alerts, and verifies.

01

Collect

Messages stream in from all connected sources in real time.

02

Classify

Keyword rules catch instantly. LLM scores for nuance and context.

03

Alert

Telegram push, email digest, or voice readout. Configurable per group.

04

Verify

Alert hashes stored on-chain via AlertAttestation.sol on Monad.
