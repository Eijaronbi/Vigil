Connect your channels.

Link WhatsApp Business, Telegram bots, Twitter API, and Gmail in one place. OAuth handles authentication. Once connected, Vigil starts listening silently in the background. No configuration files to edit, no servers to provision.

01 Connect

Classify at the edge.

Incoming messages pass through an AI classifier that assigns a priority score and content category. The model runs on-device or in the cloud. Training data is optional for common use cases. Every classification includes a confidence measure.

02 Classify
"The most important messages find you. Everything else waits." Vigil voice mode

Voice alerts. No screen.

High-priority messages trigger voice alerts through your device speakers. Say "check messages" to hear a summary of everything that arrived while you were away. Commands like "attest last urgent" work hands-free.

03 Alert

Attest on Monad.

Selected messages are hashed and submitted to Monad testnet. Each batch creates a permanent, verifiable record. No per-message gas cost at this stage. Attestations are public and can be referenced from any explorer.

04 Attest
