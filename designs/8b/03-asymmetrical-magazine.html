The pipeline.

From raw message to on-chain attestation in four deliberate stages. Each step is auditable and configurable.

01

Connect

Link your accounts through OAuth or bot tokens. Supports WhatsApp Business, Telegram bots, Twitter API v2, and Gmail labels.

02

Classify

Messages are analyzed by an on-device or cloud AI model. Each message gets a priority score and a category tag. Training optional.

03

Alert

Critical messages trigger voice or push alerts. The "check messages" voice command reads pending summaries aloud. Configurable per source.

04

Attest

Selected messages are hashed and submitted to Monad testnet. Attestations are public, verifiable, and gas-free at this stage.
