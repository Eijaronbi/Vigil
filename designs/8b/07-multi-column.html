The pipeline.

Four stages from raw message to on-chain proof.

01

Connect

One-time OAuth per source. WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter, Gmail. Ready in seconds.

02

Classify

AI tags each message by urgency and category. On-device or cloud inference.

03

Alert

Voice alerts for high-priority items. "Check messages" reads summaries out loud.

04

Attest

Messages hashed and submitted to Monad testnet. Permanent and verifiable.
