Four moves,
one system.

From channel to chain in four deliberate steps.

Connect

Link your accounts once. OAuth flows for WhatsApp Business, Telegram bot, Twitter API, and Gmail. Zero config required.

Classify

AI reads every incoming message and tags it by intent, sender, and priority. Training is optional — the model works out of the box.

Alert

High-priority messages trigger voice alerts on your device. Say "check messages" to hear summaries without touching a screen.

Attest

Selected messages are hashed and attested on Monad testnet. A permanent record with no per-message gas cost.
