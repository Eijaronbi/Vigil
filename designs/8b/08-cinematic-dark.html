The sequence.

Four deliberate stages from connection to proof.

Connect

OAuth for each source. WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter, Gmail linked in one session.

Classify

AI reads every inbound message. Priority score and category assigned automatically.

Alert

Voice alerts for critical items. Say "check messages" for audio summary.

Attest

Hash and submit to Monad testnet. Public, verifiable, gas-free per message.
