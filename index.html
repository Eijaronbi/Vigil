The Problem

Your important messages are buried in noise

You're in 10+ group chats across WhatsApp and Telegram. You follow key accounts on Twitter. Your inbox is full. Somewhere in the noise is a message that matters. A deal. A deadline. An opportunity. You can't watch everything at once.

⚠

Information Overload

Hundreds of messages daily across groups. The signal-to-noise ratio is impossible to manage manually.

⏳

Delayed Response

By the time you scroll back to find that important message, the opportunity has passed.

🔑

Fragmented Monitoring

No single tool watches WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter, and email together. You tab-hop constantly.

The Solution

Vigil watches everything so you don't have to

A unified monitoring engine that plugs into your existing accounts. It classifies every message with AI and routes important ones to you however you want. Telegram, email, or spoken aloud.

AlertAttestation.sol
On-Chain Component

Every alert is attested,
not just sent

When an important alert fires, its fingerprint is permanently recorded on Monad testnet. Anyone can verify that you were alerted about a specific message at a specific time.

AlertAttestation.sol Monad Testnet
messageHash keccak256(text | group | timestamp)
groupName string — e.g. "Deals Group"
timestamp block.timestamp when attested
importanceScore uint8 — 0 to 10
function verify(bytes32 messageHash) returns (bool)
Tamper-Proof Once written, attestations are immutable. The blockchain is your receipt.
Publicly Verifiable Anyone can call verify() with a message hash to confirm it was flagged.
No Token Required Owner-only attest function. No user wallets or gas fees for monitored users.
